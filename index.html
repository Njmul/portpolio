Biographic Sketch

Dr. Elarabi has received his Doctor of Philosophy Degree in the field of Computer Engineering from the Center for Advanced Computer Studies (CACS), University of Louisiana at Lafayette. Dr. Elarabi has been a faculty member of Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at California State University, Fresno since 2014. His expertise is in digital video processing with particular interest in video compression algorithms, and heterogeneous video transcoding for IoT real-time video applications.
